KATIKKIRO CHARLES PETER MAYIGA HAILS DR. FRANK NDUGGA FOR HEALTHCARE EXCELLENCE

By Sammy Serwadda | Thursday 26 February, 2026

Owek. Charles Peter Mayiga (black coat) inspects Peoples Medical Hospital.

GAYAZA — People’s Medical Hospital was officially opened by Owek. Charles Peter Mayiga, the Katikkiro of Buganda, in a grand celebration attended by hundreds of residents and dignitaries.

The Katikkiro and his delegation began the event by inspecting the facilities. He expressed his thrill at seeing "up-to-date technology and a touch of class" in patient handling. To commemorate the day, the Katikkiro planted a tree in the hospital compound, symbolizing growth and life.

"I didn't come here simply because of launching the hospital. I came because Dr. Frank Ndugga has always been obediently responding to the Kabaka's call of providing quality medical services to his people... As Buganda Kingdom, our theme is to improve the quality of service. I am seeing this happening here."
— Owek. Charles Peter Mayiga

A United Community Effort

The event was a gathering of power and local leadership. Stakeholders applauded Dr. Frank for his patience in growing the hospital from a small clinic into a major health center.

THE GENESIS: "A Branch off Through Bulamu"

During his speech, Dr. Frank Ndugga shared the inspiring story of how the hospital started. While working at Mulago Hospital, he often faced heavy traffic jam on the Gayaza-Kampala road. One day, he branched off through Bulamu to escape the traffic—and that is where he saw the space that would become the clinic.

"My wife and I planned to start elsewhere, but when we found this place, we chose to stay near home," Dr. Frank narrated. He expressed deep gratitude to his mother, the staff, and the Buganda Kingdom for their continued partnership.

People’s Medical Hospital now stands as the very first hospital of its standard in the Gayaza corridor, setting a new history for healthcare in Kasangati Town Council.
