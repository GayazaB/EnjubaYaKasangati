About Our School

Founded on strong Anglican traditions, Gayaza C.O.U. S.S. is a pillar of academic and spiritual growth in Gayaza. We provide an environment where every student is challenged to lead and excel.

Academic Excellence

Our academic record speaks for itself. With modern Science and Computer labs, we prepare students for the demands of high-level higher education and the modern professional world.

Vocational Training

We believe in "Education for Self-Reliance." Students participate in Tailoring, Comuter Science Courses, Electricity and Agriculture projects, giving them skills for Afreneurship.

School Clubs: Boys & Girls Brigade

Discipline is our core value. Through the boys and girls Brigade and other students' clubs, we build character, physical fitness, creativity and community spirit.
