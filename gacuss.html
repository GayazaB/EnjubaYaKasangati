About the School

Gayaza Church of Uganda Senior Secondary School is a government-aided Church-founded school located in the heart of Gayaza. We provide a balanced education that caters to both spiritual growth and academic excellence.

Academic Excellence

Our school is renowned for strong performance in both Science and Arts subjects. We offer a wide range of vocational subjects alongside the standard UNEB curriculum, ensuring our students are ready for the modern workforce.
