S.4 JOY CONTINUES: Gayaza Church of Uganda SS Shines in UCE Results

Published on February 20, 2026 | By Sammy Serwadda

Pure joy: 2025 candidates celebrate their hard-earned success at the school campus.

457 / 3457 National Rank
100% Division 1
1st Class Overall Grade

GAYAZA — The atmosphere at Gayaza Church of Uganda Secondary School was one of immense gratitude and triumph on Friday, February 20th, as the 2025 UCE candidates returned to campus to celebrate their exceptional performance.

In a heartwarming gesture of appreciation, the former Senior Four students organized a surprise "Cake Party" for their teachers. Arriving without prior notice, the students brought cake and refreshments to thank the staff for their tireless dedication during the academic year.

"We wanted to show our teachers that their long hours and extra lessons did not go in vain," said one of the former candidates. "This success belongs to them as much as it belongs to us."

Rising in the National Rankings

According to the school Headteacher, Mr. Wamusai, the school's performance has significantly boosted its standing among educational institutions in Uganda. Out of the 3,457 schools nationwide that sat for the UCE exams, Gayaza C.U. SS was ranked number 457 among those with the best grades.

The most remarkable achievement of the year was the consistency of the results: every single learner at the school attained a Result 1 grade, cementing the school's reputation as a hub for academic excellence in Kasangati Town Council.

A sweet thank you: Students and teachers share a moment of joy during the surprise party.

Mr. Wamusai attributed the success to a "Kingdom-minded" approach to education, emphasizing discipline, spiritual growth, and academic rigor. As the new term begins, the school looks forward to maintaining this momentum with the current candidates.
